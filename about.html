About Us

Welcome to Café Corner, a place where great coffee meets comfort. We started our journey in 2010, and since then, we've been dedicated to offering high-quality coffee, fresh pastries, and an inviting atmosphere for our guests.

Our café is not just a place to get your daily dose of caffeine, but a place where memories are made, laughter is shared, and new friendships are forged. Come experience the best of Café Corner, your go-to spot for a perfect coffee break.

Our Coffee Specialties

Espresso

A strong, bold shot of pure coffee, ideal for coffee lovers who crave intensity.

Cappuccino

A perfect mix of espresso, steamed milk, and a creamy foam topping for a balanced taste.

Latte

Espresso combined with steamed milk, topped with a thin layer of frothy foam for a smooth experience.

Mocha

Indulge in the combination of rich chocolate and espresso, with a hint of whipped cream on top.

Cold Brew

A chilled, smooth coffee brewed slowly over 12 hours for a less acidic and refreshing taste.

Flat White

A creamy blend of espresso and steamed milk, offering a velvety smooth texture with a rich flavor.

Our Timings

Open every day from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Come visit us anytime for your favorite coffee and snacks!
